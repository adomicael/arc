Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What Arc Collects

Arc stores all your data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Goals, plans, and mandates you create
  • Time tracking sessions
  • Notes and preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you use the AI chat feature.

AI Chat

When you use the per-goal AI assistant, your messages and goal context are sent to a Supabase Edge Function, which forwards them to OpenAI for processing. These messages are used solely to generate a response and are not stored on our servers.

OpenAI may process your messages according to their own privacy policy. No conversation history is stored server-side by Arc.

Anonymous Authentication

Arc creates an anonymous device-level session via Supabase Auth for rate limiting purposes. This session:

  • Contains no personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Is not linked to any email, phone number, or account
  • Is used solely to prevent abuse of the AI chat service

Notifications

Arc may request permission to send local notifications for daily nudges and per-goal reminders. All notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No push notification infrastructure or server is involved.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Edge Functions hosting and anonymous auth. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — AI chat processing. Privacy Policy

Arc does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally. Deleting the Arc app from your device permanently deletes all your data. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.
