FAQ

Where is my data stored?

All your data — goals, plans, mandates, time logs — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. Nothing is synced to a server. Deleting the app permanently deletes all your data.

How does the AI chat work?

When you use the AI assistant, your messages and goal context are sent to a secure server-side function that forwards them to OpenAI. No conversation history is stored on our servers. The AI can advise and reflect, but it never modifies your plans or data.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data lives on your device, deleting the Arc app removes everything permanently. There is no server-side data, no account, and nothing to request deletion of.
